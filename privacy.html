Privacy Policy

Home > Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Five Group. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and organizational data. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website (myfivegroup.com) and utilize our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect information directly from you when you submit a query, request a consultation, apply for a job position, or engage with our services. This information may include:

  • Contact Information: Name, professional email address, phone number, and physical office address.
  • Organizational Details: Company name, size, business domain, and technical requirements.
  • Employment Information: Resumes, employment history, portfolios, and details submitted via our careers form.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and tracking details through cookies to optimize your site experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

Five Group utilizes the information we collect to operate efficiently, build solutions, and communicate effectively. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Provide, update, and secure our software and simulator services.
  • Respond to your contact inquiries, schedule calls, and send proposals.
  • Process employment applications and evaluate candidates for our careers program.
  • Analyze website performance and visitor behavior to optimize the website layout and user interface.
  • Comply with regulatory, legal, and audit obligations.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal or business data to third parties. We may share information only in the following controlled contexts:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party partners who perform technical processes (e.g., hosting, email delivery) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: When compelled by law, court orders, or law enforcement investigations to protect safety, intellectual property, or prevent fraud.

5. Data Security

We implement a range of administrative, physical, and technical security measures (including SSL encryption across our domains and strict database access controls) to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute safety.

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, edit, restrict, or delete your personal information stored with us. If you wish to make a request regarding your data, please contact our data team at info@myfivegroup.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will take effect immediately upon posting on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
